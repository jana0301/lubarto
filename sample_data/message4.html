CELEBRATE THE 30TH ANNUAL FRIENDSHIP POWWOW ON FREE FIRST SATURDAY
September 7, 10 am–5 pm
Join us for the 30th Annual Friendship Powwow and American Indian Cultural Celebration featuring dancers, drum groups, and more. In addition to the powwow performances, you can enjoy hands-on art activities and explore the Paint Studio and exhibitions inside the museum.

Powwow activities and general museum admission are free for everyone.  THE FIRST SATURDAY OF THE MONTH IS ALWAYS FREE FOR FAMILIES!!

 

FOXY AND SHMOXY: ART DETECTIVES ARE BACK
September 8, 10:30 & 11:30 am
Bring your whole family for the return performance of Foxy and Shmoxy. Help the super-sleuths sniff out clues and solve art mysteries in the museum. Foxy and Shmoxy are back in action on the second Sunday of each month through May.
Foxy and Shmoxy performances and activities are included in general admission, which is free for youth 18 and younger every day.
